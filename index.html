• Section One

    It just happens - whether or not you worried about it or tried to plan it. We have a fantastic little sky! Let all these little things happen. Don't fight them. Learn to use them. We'll throw some old gray clouds in here just sneaking around and having fun.

  • Section Two

    We can always carry this a step further. There's really no end to this. I thought today we would make a happy little stream that's just running through the woods here. We don't make mistakes we just have happy little accidents.

  • Section Three

    You can work and carry-on and put lots of little happy things in here. That's why I paint - because I can create the kind of world I want - and I can make this world as happy as I want it. In your world you can create anything you desire. Now it's beginning to make a little sense. Put your feelings into it, your heart, it's your world. This is an example of what you can do with just a few things, a little imagination and a happy dream in your heart.

  • Section Four

    Just use the old one inch brush. Just let these leaves jump off the brush You could sit here for weeks with your one hair brush trying to do that - or you could do it with one stroke with an almighty brush. Isn't that fantastic? We tell people sometimes: we're like drug dealers, come into town and get everybody absolutely addicted to painting. It doesn't take much to get you addicted.
